Songfest 2020: Run Forrest Run

This year, ACE is excited to partner with Aggie Sweethearts! With the uncertainty COVID-19 has brought, we are looking forward to carrying on this annual tradition with one of our favorite women's organizations. While we hope to enjoy this glimpse of normalcy, ACE still keeps Songfest's overarching mission at heart: giving back. ACE will be supporting Camp Hope and the Sweethearts will be partnering with the Hill Country Children's Advocacy Center during this amazing charity event. We are looking forward to building lasting relationships and making a lasting impact for our charities of choice.

How can you help?

Donate!

Corporations and individuals alike can donate directly through Songfest. If you are interested in that method of donation, click the link above. If you would prefer to work with ACE or Camp Hope directly, contact our service chair for more information!

Attend the show!

Just like t-shirt sales, each ticket purchase benefits the Songfest philanthropies and the team you are supporting! No matter what team you choose to support, we would love to see you at Songfest 2020!

Purchase a T-shirt!

All proceeds from t-shirt sales benefit Songfest's philanthropic partners. Not only that, but each t-shirt sale adds points to the act of your choice!
