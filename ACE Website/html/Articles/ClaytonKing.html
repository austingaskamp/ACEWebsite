Clayton King: SpaceX Mission Control

As everybody in the United States was watching the livestream of the Crew Dragon Demo-2 Mission (DM-2), behind the scenes was ACE’s very own alumni Clayton King ‘15 hard at work! “My role in the DM-2 mission was supporting the CRONUS console, from Mission Control Center in Houston, as a flight controller for the International Space Station. I was on console for the shifts after the launch and after the docking. The CRONUS console supports the command, data handling, communication, and tracking systems for the ISS. Ahead of the mission, I worked closely with our DM-2 team to develop several audio procedures for voice between DM-2 and ISS.” Congratulations Clayton on a successful mission and all the best for what lies ahead!
